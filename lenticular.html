LENTICULARS


2023 - Lenticular prints, hair, plant matter

Three lenticular diptychs:

  • A lenticular print depicting a photograph of myself being dressed up by my family as a barbie "bride" in the 1990's and a photograph from my grandmother's marriage at age twelve in 1933. The frame is decorated with plant matter and hair from myself, my mother, and grandmother.

  • A lenticular print depicting a photograph of my grandmother holding my mother as a baby and a photograph of my mother holding me.The frame is decorated with plant matter and hair from myself, my mother, and grandmother.

  • A lenticular print depicting a photograph of my grandmother and her sister from 2022 and a photograph of my grandmother and her sister in the 1970s. The frame is decorated with plant matter and hair from my grandmother.
